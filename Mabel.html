Who is Mabel Pines?

She is a 12-year-old girl spending the summer with her Great Uncle Stan in Gravity Falls, Oregon, where she and her brother frequently encounter the "supernatural". She takes a much less serious approach to life than her twin brother, as she navigates her way around odd, new surroundings.

Armed with a Grappling Hook, she and her brother seeks to uncover the town's mysterious secrets.
