Who is Dipper Pines?

He's a smart, curious and adventurous 12-Year-Old boy spending his summer in Gravity Falls, Oregon, where he and his sister (Mabel Pines) encounter the town's"paranormal"tendencies.

Armed with a "mysterious"journal he found in a hidden place inside the forest, he is the overall main protagonist of Gravity Falls and seeks to uncover the town's mysterious secrets.
