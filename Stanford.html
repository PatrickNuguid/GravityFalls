Who is Stanford Pines?

A paranormal investigator who came to Gravity Falls, Oregon, to study the huge concentration of supernatural activity in and around the town. After spending years of cataloging his research in a series of journals, he disappeared into an alternate dimension. His writings were left as the only evidence of his existence. Until his brother (Stanley Pines) repaired the portal and brought him back.

With his Brain, Inventions, and Experience, he helped his family put "Gravity Falls" back to normal.
