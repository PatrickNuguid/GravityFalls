Who is Stanley Pines?

Dipper and Mabel Pines' great uncle and summer guardian. After many years of crime and infamy, he has taken up residence in the remote town of Gravity Falls, Oregon, where he exploits local lore and the gullibility of the "dumbest people in the world" to finance himself by running a dubious tourist trap known as the Mystery Shack.

With his hat and negotiation skill, he helps Dipper and Mabel uncover the Town's "paranormal" and later rescue his own twin brother.
